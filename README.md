# TrailForge IT Infrastructure Project

## Overview
Designed a scalable SaaS-first IT infrastructure and systems environment for a fast-growing e-commerce company targeting expansion from $5M to $30M in revenue.

## Business Problem
The organization lacked a production-ready network, identity platform, and secure commerce infrastructure for a new facility. Without a structured solution, growth would introduce operational risk, security gaps, and inefficiencies.

## Solution
Developed a SaaS-first hybrid architecture combining cloud-based platforms with a right-sized on-site network to support secure, scalable business operations.

## Key Components
- Hosted e-commerce and business platforms
- Secure network infrastructure (LAN, WiFi, firewall)
- Identity and access management with MFA
- Endpoint protection and centralized logging
- Backup and recovery strategy

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Systems Analysis
- IT Project Planning
- Business Requirements Development
- Risk Management and Mitigation
- Cost Estimation and Budgeting
- Security Framework Alignment (NIST, PCI DSS)

## Tools & Technologies
- PowerPoint
- SaaS Platforms
- Network Design Concepts

## Deliverables
- Comprehensive Project Plan
- Executive Presentation

## Outcome
Delivered a structured, scalable IT solution aligned with business growth objectives while minimizing operational risk and controlling costs.

## Business Impact
Enabled a secure, scalable technology foundation to support rapid growth, improve customer experience, and reduce operational risk.
